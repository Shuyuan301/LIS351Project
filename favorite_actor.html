Jackie Chan

Image: NeverGrowUp Book Cover by Simon and Schuster at https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:NeverGrowUp_Book_Cover.jpg, CC BY-SA 2.0.

My favorite actor is Jackie Chan. He is a Hong Kong actor. He is also a filmmaker, singer and even a writer. He is well-known all over the world by his fighting style, and excellent acting with a comedy style. He practiced Kung Fu when he was young, and he started acting since around the 1960s. He is a great actor, which has performed in more over 150 films. The reason why I like him is his attitude for life and work. He is always humble and hardworking. I admire him, and I hope I can see him one day.
